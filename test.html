How much do you know about the Live Virtual Format at this point in time? It's okay that you don't know all of the ins and outs just yet. Try your hand at these three True/False questions and then we will cover more details below.

All content is covered in the live session with students.

Answer

False. The majority of your lecture materials (depending on the nature of the course and % of synchronous time), videos, and supporting notes will be in Canvas and should be viewed by students before the live class
The live part of class is for collaborative active learning, sharing of relevant professional experiences, live discussion, etc.

Answer

True. You will facilitate group activities and active learning assignments during the live session. You can also use this time to share some of your professional experiences from the field.
Each course will be pre-populated with content.

Answer

True. Lecture notes, videos, knowledge checks, etc. will be built in to each section.
