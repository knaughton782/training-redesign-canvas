Welcome

Welcome to the New Online Instructor Preparation course. We are here to help you be ready to teach online for Webster University. You will find five modules of information full of content to help you be an engaging and interactive instructor in one of our totally asynchronous online courses.

This training opportunity is offered to you in the weeks before you get access to your first online course section. By the end of the session, you should be ready to prepare your upcoming course for students. But we know you will still have questions. We are here for you every step of the way.

  1. At any time, you can access the Knowledge Center to get more in depth information about a variety of topics.
  2. In the week of or before student load, you will have the opportunity to meet with your training partner in a one-on-one or small group Zoom session to go over your course and ask questions. Details will be available in an announcement.
  3. The Faculty Support for Teaching (FaST) group publishes a newsletter monthly and posts the articles in Connections.
  4. FaST also offers Open Office Hours weekly, on Tuesdays (noon - 5:00 pm CT) and Thursdays (9:00 - 5:00 pm CT).
