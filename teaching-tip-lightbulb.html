Teaching Tip

Keep track of the introduction details on a spreadsheet (download a copy of the gradebook on Day 1: Learn How)

A spreadsheet can help you to keep track of individual student details so you can refer to them as the course progresses.

Teaching Tip

Keep track of the announcements you create in your own Word doc. If you teach the course again, you might be able to reuse some or all of the information you provided on a weekly basis.

Tech Tips

You can see if a student has dropped your course if their name disappears from the “People” tab in the Course Navigation or is no longer in your grade book.

Use the Course Analytics (found on the right side of the Course Home Page) to get an overview of student participation. Scroll down to the bottom of the page and then click on a student name to get started. The charts will indicate page views, participation, communication, and grades. You can scroll through the list of students by using the bar at the top of the screen. If you view the list of students from the People link, you can click on the individual names to see the specific pages each student has accessed.
