Expectations for Training Completion

Once you are added to this course, you are expected, by the OLC and your department, to complete it within two (2) weeks and at least two (2) weeks before students are loaded. You will need this time to review the course content and assignments and make any necessary adjustments.

The content review should take about two hours of your time.

The final module, Completion of Training, will only open after you have completed all of the module requirements.
